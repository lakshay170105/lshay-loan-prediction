Learn about Lakshay Loan Prediction and the technology behind it

🎯 Our Mission

Lakshay Loan Prediction is an AI-powered loan approval prediction system built to help individuals understand their loan eligibility before approaching a bank. We use machine learning to give you a realistic picture of your approval chances.

🤖 The Technology

Our system trains 7 supervised ML algorithms — Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, KNN, SVM, and Naive Bayes — on 1000+ realistic loan records. The best-performing model (Random Forest at 89% accuracy) is used for predictions.

📊 What We Analyze
  • Credit Score — your financial trustworthiness
  • Annual Income — repayment capacity
  • Loan-to-Income Ratio — risk assessment
  • Job Experience — employment stability
  • Age — loan tenure suitability
  • Existing Loans — current liability
  • Education Level — profile strength
  • Marital Status — financial responsibility
⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is built for educational purposes as part of a BCA Machine Learning project. Predictions are based on a synthetic dataset and should not be used as actual financial advice. Always consult a certified financial advisor before applying for a loan.
