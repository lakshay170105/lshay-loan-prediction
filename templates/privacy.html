Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Lakshay Loan Prediction, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data you enter into the prediction form. All inputs are processed locally on the server and discarded immediately after the prediction is returned.

2. Cookies

This site uses minimal session cookies required for Flask to function. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for details.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts and Chart.js CDN for UI rendering. These services may log your IP address as part of their standard operations. We do not share any form data with third parties.

4. Security

All data is processed server-side. No prediction data is written to disk or databases. See our Security Policy for more.

5. Educational Use

This platform is built for educational purposes. It is not a financial service and does not process real loan applications.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, visit our Contact page.
